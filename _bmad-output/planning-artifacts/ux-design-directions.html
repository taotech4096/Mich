Direction 4: Split Personality

Homepage splits into the two product lines — Lebanese (green) and Greek (blue) — as entry points. Below the split, featured products appear in a unified Mediterranean layout with horizontal cards. Macros strip for fitness audience. Best for: users who know what they want, category navigation.
